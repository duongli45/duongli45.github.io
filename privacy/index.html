Privacy Policy - Scroll Escaper

Last Updated: March 20, 2026

Developer: Li Nguyen

1. Overview

Scroll Escaper is a screen time management app that helps you limit social media usage through playful challenges. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect and how it is used.

2. Data Stored on Your Device

All core app data — including blocked app lists, challenge history, usage statistics, and settings — is stored locally on your device using an on-device database. This data is never transmitted to external servers.

3. Analytics Data We Collect

We use Firebase Analytics (provided by Google) to understand how the app is used and improve the experience. Firebase Analytics collects the following data anonymously:

Usage Data (Product Interaction):

Screen views and navigation patterns

Feature usage events (e.g., monitoring started/stopped, challenges started/completed, settings changed)

No personally identifiable information is included in these events

Device Identifiers:

Firebase automatically generates an anonymous app instance ID to distinguish sessions. This is not linked to your identity and is not shared with third parties for tracking purposes.

Diagnostics:

Basic app performance data such as app version, OS version, and session duration.

This data is NOT linked to your identity, NOT used for advertising, and NOT shared with third parties beyond Google's Firebase service.

4. HealthKit Data

Scroll Escaper accesses Apple HealthKit to read step count data for the Walking Challenge feature. This data is:

Processed locally on your device

Step counts may be included in anonymous analytics events to measure challenge completion

Never sold or shared with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes

Used solely for app functionality and analytics

5. What We Do NOT Collect

Personal information (name, email, phone number)

Location data

Photos or media (camera is used only for real-time Object Hunter challenge — no images are stored or transmitted)

Contacts or address book data

Financial or payment information

Advertising identifiers (IDFA)

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated through the app. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
